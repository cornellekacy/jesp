Business Support

Give your business the tools to succeed
Do you wish to learn and implement best practices that will transform your business?

JESF finance and business support work hand-in-hand to help your business develop capacity, thus improving its turnover and profits. JESF STEP also helps you enhance your management skills towards developing growth strategies as well as managing and mitigating risks. This makes your business more viable and competitive, allowing you to make a mark in your community through job creation and improvement in the livelihoods of those around you.

How do we maximise the benefit of our support to your business?

 We provide you with a dedicated Investment           Manager

Your JESF Investment Manager is both financier and mentor to your business. Your Investment Manager acts as a single point of contact for business support, offering you with hands-on mentorship and a sounding board where you have a short-term orientation or lack an independent, neutral perspective on your business.

 We provide you with the services of our local      partners & SME stakeholders

We connect you with industry stakeholders, where possible, to provide the benefits of synergy to your business. To ensure best-in-class technical assistance, we refer you to our local service providers or experts for Accounting services, Legal services, Technical/Operational Advice, Expert Sales and Marketing Consultancy, and IT Services.

 We leverage in-house industry expertise

We bring to you the services of our in-house industry experts in specific sectors to support your business based on their many years of experience and in-depth knowledge of these industries – covering education, healthcare, manufacturing, retail, food and beverages, transportation, construction, accommodation, fuel retail, administrative and support services, mining and quarrying, among others.

 We keep your business on track with continuous    health checks

We conduct quarterly checks on your business, which allow us to detect any areas that require attention and help you address them in a timely and efficient manner.
