Our sectors of focus

While we are open to funding SMEs across sectors on the understanding that consumers at the base of the pyramid need access to a complete range of goods and services, JESF focuses on SGBs operating in education, healthcare, agri-business, manufacturing and key services (water, energy and sanitation). JESF targets these vital needs sectors because of the impact these businesses have in directly working towards the SDGs and thus their capacity to alleviate poverty.
